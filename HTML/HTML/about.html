All about me, Lucas Snow

Thank you for choosing my website Winter Wonderscapes to search for you're next winter holiday. Here you will learn a bit about myself and my life on the snow!
I am 26 years old and was born in a town in colorado, and a 20 minute drive one of the best snow resorts in America. I started snowboarding when I was 3 years old and have won many awards at various competitions across the world.
I obtained my snowboarding instructor certificate when I was just 16 and have been helping people learn ever since. I created this website in order to reach a broader audience to help that I couldn't reach before.

This was me in St Anton am Arlberg, Austria, 2017, this is rated as the best ski resort in all of austria. In the picture you see me about to go down the hardest trail on the mountain for the first time.
