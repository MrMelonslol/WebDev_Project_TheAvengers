This is Niseko, a top-rated destination on our website. Nestled on Japan's northern island of Hokkaido, it's famous for its vast ski resort, offering diverse trails for beginners to seasoned pros. Renowned for its powder snow, Niseko is a paradise for winter sports enthusiasts and nature lovers alike. A truly ideal winter wonderland."


Client story


Niseko has always been a hugely popular tourist destination. Last winter, one of our clients visited solo, driving herself through the scenic roads to Hokkaido and spending nine days exploring in January. She absolutely loved it, recommending Niseko highly, as she made lifelong friends there and is already planning her next winter holiday with us.
